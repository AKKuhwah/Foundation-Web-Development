Workout

Regular exercise is essential as it improves physical health, mental well-being, and overall quality of life.

Arms
  • Bicep Curls - Target: Front of the upper arm (biceps)
  • Tricep Dips - Target: Back of the upper arm (triceps)
  • Hammer Curls - Target: Front of the upper arm (biceps) and forearm muscles
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions - Target: Back of the upper arm (triceps)
  • Reverse Curls - Target: Underside of the forearm and biceps
Leg
  • Squats - Target: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes
  • Lunges - Target: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes
  • Deadlifts - Target: Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back
  • Calf Raises - Target: Calves (Gastrocnemius and Soleus muscles)
  • Leg Press - Target: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes
Back
  • Pull-Ups - Target: Upper back (Latissimus Dorsi), Biceps
  • Deadlifts - Target: Lower and upper back, Hamstrings, Glutes
  • Bent-Over Rows - Target: Middle and upper back (Rhomboids, Trapezius)
  • Lat Pulldowns - Target: Upper back (Latissimus Dorsi), Biceps
  • Hyperextensions - Target: Lower back, Glutes, Hamstrings
Cardio
  • Running - Benefit: Improves cardiovascular health, burns calories, strengthens lower body muscles
  • Cycling - Benefit: Enhances cardiovascular fitness, strengthens legs and glutes, low impact on joints
  • Jump Rope - Benefit: Increases cardiovascular endurance, improves coordination, burns calories
